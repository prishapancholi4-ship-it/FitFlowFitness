Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Matters

FitFlow is designed to be a personal wellness companion. We believe your data should stay in your hands, so FitFlow stores all progress, preferences, and activity logs locally on your device. No information is uploaded, transmitted, or shared with external servers.

What We Store

  • Your points, streaks, and level progress
  • Your wellness logs (sleep, water, workouts, meals, meditation, affirmations, journal entries)
  • Your onboarding status
  • Your login state

What We Do NOT Store

  • No personal identity data is collected or transmitted
  • No analytics or tracking is performed
  • No data is shared with third parties
  • No cloud storage is used

Your Control

You may clear all FitFlow data at any time by logging out or clearing your browser storage. This will reset your progress and remove all locally stored information.
